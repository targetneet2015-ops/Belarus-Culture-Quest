September Harvest

The prime season for **Boletes**. Families head into the woods to collect these prized mushrooms. In villages like Vitebsk, you will see them strung on thread and hung above clay stoves to dry.

UNESCO World Heritage Site

Straw: Guardian of the Home

*Solomopletsenne* (straw weaving) is on the UNESCO Intangible Heritage Watchlist. More than just a craft, straw is considered the "spirit of the harvest."

Did you know?

Artisans often begin training with their grandmothers as young as seven or eight. It transforms a humble material into a sacred charm.

Pavuk (Spider Ornament)

Complex, geometric shapes traditionally hung above the dining table. They act as a "guardian of the home," catching bad energy in their webs.

The "Living Heritage" Experience Package

Day 1: Village Immersion

Arrival in a traditional homestead. Introduction to host families. Evening storytelling session about local myths.

Day 2: Craft & Forest

Morning foraging walk with a local guide (seasonal harvesting). Afternoon *Solomopletsenne* (Straw Weaving) masterclass at Dudutki.

Day 3: The Kupalle Night

Preparation for the evening ritual. Wreath making. Bonfire lighting at sunset. Flame jumping and traditional songs until dawn.
